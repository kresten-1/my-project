Water Moon and the Deep Blue Tree

Placeholder overview for Water Moon and the Deep Blue Tree.

Endings

Ending 2 — "The Tranquil Age"

On floors 1–3, trigger the random encounter "Frenzied Obsession" and choose one of the following:

  1. Spend 2 Health and 2 Hope to receive the trinket "Breath of the Tide". A knight companion will join the fight.
  2. Roll the die once; on a successful roll you obtain "Breath of the Tide" and the knight joins the battle.
If the knight survives and reaches floor 5, the knight will depart and you will unlock the ending "The Tranquil Age".

Ending 3 — "The Bishop's Research"

On floors 1–3, during the random encounter "Commencement" choose "Decide to infiltrate the deep sea" to obtain the trinket "The Bishop's Research". Later, on floors 4–5 in the random encounter "Heart of the Deep Blue" choose "Search the gardens" to obtain the trinket "Heart of the Deep Blue".

Ending 4 — "The Cost of the Waning Tide"

On floors 1–3, in the "Commencement" event choose the option "Deep blue?!" to obtain the trinket "Deep‑Blue Reminiscence", which unlocks the ending "The Cost of the Waning Tide".

On floors 4–5, the random encounter "Transcendence" will present choices that award a trinket depending on your Lamp value; any of those trinkets will allow access to the Ending 3 route, while different trinkets may raise or lower the boss difficulty.
