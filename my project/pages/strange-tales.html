Endings

Ending 2 — "Ink‑ed Relic"

In the wonderland "Choice" node select "Pay" and spend 30 Source Ingots to obtain the trinket "Hidden‑Butterfly Ink". This trinket may also be acquired by a gamble. While equipped, the main map's combat stages will spawn the entity "Year‑Corpse"; when you reduce that entity to half HP it vanishes and you automatically obtain the ending trinket "Ink‑ed Relic". Selecting "Offer the Monument" at the floor‑5 "Fate's Direction" node will unlock this ending.

Ending 3 — "Cloud & Lacquer"

At a "Doubt" node within the wonderland encounter the "Chessmaster" event. If you have more than five Candlebearers, choose "Flip the board" then select "Take the board" to obtain the trinket "Forgotten Panoply". Enter the "Guidance" node and pick "Board to Flame, Script to Light" to trigger a special combat "Clarity"; after victory you receive the trinket "Cloud & Lacquer", which unlocks this ending. Note: the final boss "Schemer of Years" allows only Candlebearers to participate.

Ending 4 — "Unsealed Scroll"

On your first visit to the "Ease and Difficulty" shop you may purchase the trinket "Unsealed Long Box". Later, use a "Stride Ahead" event to have an operator enter the volume early and acquire the "Uninked Scroll", enabling access to the fourth ending route.
