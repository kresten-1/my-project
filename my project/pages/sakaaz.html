The Endless Strange Words of Sakaaz

Placeholder overview for The Endless Strange Words of Sakaaz.

Endings

Ending 2 — "Babel Oath"

On floor 2's "Fate's Direction" event obtain the Pure White Petal and the Wordless Deed. At any "Discard and Keep" node combine the Pure White Petal with the Wordless Deed to craft the trinket "Babel Tower Oath". Before the floor‑5 boss, at the "Fate's Direction" choice select the option to "Recite the Oath" to unlock this ending.

Ending 3 — "Prophet's Horn"

At any "Loss and Gain" node choose the option "A small box" to obtain the trinket "Seer's Horn". After obtaining it, refresh any node; a "Fate's Direction" event will then appear. Complete that refreshed Fate's Direction and clear the wonder to obtain the ending item "Time and Light", which grants the Ending‑3 outcome.

Ending 4 — "Anasa Kamma"

During "Loss and Gain" choose the option "A lotus petal" to receive the trinket "Petal Fragment". In the event "Narrow Path Encounter" select "Throw the petal into the story". The third wave of enemies will be two Reborn Lotus Thrones; defeat them to obtain the trinket "Anasa Kamma", unlocking this ending.

Ending 5 — "The Unending Key"

In random encounters there is a chance to meet "Sudden Void"; choose "Try to accept it" to obtain the trinket "Proclaimer's Apparition". After this, special enemies called the "Final Chorus" may appear in subsequent combat stages. Killing a "Final Chorus" yields a new thought called Despair. If the enemy survives, it deals 1 HP damage and drops a random trinket while continuing to the next stage.

Combine thoughts: Despair + Conception → Salvation; Salvation + Conception → Stopping. These three thoughts correspond to difficulty variants of Ending‑5 (Despair < Salvation < Stopping). When you hold any of these thoughts and later encounter the event "All‑or‑Nothing", choose "Forge a key through transformation" and sacrifice the thought to receive a debuff trinket and the Ending‑5 item "Endless Key", unlocking the ending. Clearing a run with any ending also unlocks the trinket "Ink‑ed Relic".
