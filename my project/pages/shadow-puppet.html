Shadow Puppet and Crimson Blood Diamond

Placeholder overview for Shadow Puppet and Crimson Blood Diamond.

Endings

Ending 2 — "Absurd Comedy"

On floors 1–3, when you trigger the random encounter "Relief", choose the option "How pitiful — let us watch closely" to obtain the trinket "Tattered Doll". This choice unlocks the ending titled "Absurd Comedy".

Ending 3 — "Scapegoat & Performance"

On floors 1–3, during the random encounter "Banquet" pick the option "Take a white outfit" to obtain the trinket "Scapegoat Scarf". After the floor‑3 boss fight, select the trinket "Stand‑in Actor" to progress this route.

Ending 4 — "Grand Reveal" / "Silent Chapter"

On floors 4–5 the random encounter "Follower Gathering": choose "Push the door and enter" to receive the trinket "Epiphany", which unlocks the ending "Grand Reveal".

Alternatively, for the more clandestine route: on floors 1–3 choose "Do not disturb them" then "Listen closely to what they say" to trigger the combat "Self‑Binding"; after victory you receive the trinket "Dance of the Condemned". Later, the floors 4–5 random encounter "Revenge Play" with the choice "See what they're writing" leads to the combat "Observation"; after that you obtain the trinket "First Act", unlocking the ending "Silent Chapter".
