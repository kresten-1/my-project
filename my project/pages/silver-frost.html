The Explorer's Silver Frost End

Placeholder overview for The Explorer's Silver Frost End.

Endings

Ending 2 — "Tree‑Scar's Helm"

During the "Loss and Gain" event choose "Seek the trail of the tree‑scar" to obtain a set of special ciphertext plates. Use these plates to perform the proclamation ritual and obtain the trinket "Tree‑Scar Helm". On floor 5 at the "Fate's Direction" event choose "Wear the Tree‑Scar Helm" to trigger this ending.

Ending 3 — "The Boundless Gift"

In the "Stride Ahead" event select the trinket "Road Network". The next floor will spawn the "Fate's Direction" event, where you can obtain the Ending‑3 trinket "Boundless Gift", leading to this ending.

Ending 4 — "Cost of Fate"

Purchase the trinket "Invitation to the Sight" from shops on floors 1–3 (cost: 1 coin). In a random encounter titled "Change", present the "Invitation to the Sight" as your option; after winning the ensuing fight you will receive the trinket "Dimensional Flux".

After clearing floor 5, on floor 6 the "Fate's Direction" event offers three successive choices:

  1. Ignore collapse pollution → receive negative trinket "Seed of Collapse" (−15% attack and HP for allies).
  2. Overload the detector (cost: 3 Interference Resist).
Then next choices may consume resources (source ingots / tree‑scar helm / hope) or grant negative trinkets; any of these can be offset by using the trinket "Anma's Love". These choices affect difficulty and outcomes; choose wisely to complete the Ending‑4 route.
